Matchmaker: Matching Producers and Consumers in a Vector Space?

In addition to possible technical advantages this project possesses as a result of including members who have an advanced understanding of site design and reliability engineering, there are also potential advantage from an advanced model of matching producers with consumers. In particular, we have already done work, in an agent-based-modeling context, on using a vector-space model to match agents with other agents. We feel this holds great promise for producer-consumer matching, as it would allow matching along many dimensions, each given a weighting dependant upon the importance the consumer gives to that dimension.
